## Multi-Cat Specific Research Notes

### Litter consumption rates
- 1 cat: ~7-10 lbs/month with regular scooping
- 2 cats: ~15-20 lbs/month
- 3 cats: ~25-30 lbs/month
- Budget impact: at 3 cats, cost/lb difference between Dr. Elsey's ($0.52) and BoxiePro ($1.50) is $25-30/month

### Box management consensus (Reddit + vet advice)
- Rule: N+1 boxes (2 cats = 3 boxes, 3 cats = 4 boxes)
- More boxes = more litter consumed but better odor distribution
- Multi-cat owners consistently report odor is the #1 complaint
- Second complaint: refill cost/frequency

### Odor under load
- Owner reports split: fragrance-forward litters (A&H Platinum) work fast but some cats reject them
- Probiotic/enzymatic approaches (BoxiePro, World's Best) work slower but build over time
- Unscented options (Dr. Elsey's) rely on clump seal quality — if clumps break, odor escapes

### Clump stability under traffic
- More cats = more paw traffic through the box = more clump breakage
- Hard-clumping formulas (Dr. Elsey's, BoxiePro) hold together better
- Lighter granule litters (World's Best, some Tidy Cats) clump less firmly
