## Cross-source consensus signals

Patterns that show up across 3+ independent tier-1/tier-2 sources (cats.com + catster.com + CNN Underscored):

- **Litter-Robot 4** anchors most roundups as the reliability benchmark, with cats.com, catster, and CNN all giving it top or near-top placement per the captured screenshots.
- **PetKit PuraMax 2** recurs as the value-premium alternative to the Litter-Robot for multi-cat apartment buyers.
- **PetSafe ScoopFree Smart / Ultra** is the reliably-cheaper mid-tier pick for people who do not want a rotating globe.
- **Leo's Loo Too** is the UV-odor + smart-home-integration pick; NBC News Select singled it out specifically for Alexa/Google Home support.
- **Neakasa M1** is the open-top pick for cats that reject enclosed globes.

## Failure-mode themes (tier-3 weighted, for skeptical framing)

From r/litterrobot + r/CatAdvice threads + Chewy reviews (used for framing the cons sections, not as primary authority):

- Subscription and consumable lock-in on Leo's Loo Too waste-bag refills.
- App-reliability complaints on CATLINK and PetKit WiFi pairing post-firmware updates.
- Motor-reliability complaints on Pet Zone Smart Scoop after 12-18 months of daily use.
- Cat-refusal is a meaningful risk on the globe-shaped models (Litter-Robot 4, PetKit PuraMax); open-top designs (Neakasa, PETPIVOT) de-risk that failure mode.

## Final-five candidates (Phase 2 preview, not locked)

Based on consensus + desk fit + price-tier coverage for a single roundup:

1. **Litter-Robot 4** — best overall, reliability benchmark, multi-cat ready.
2. **PetKit PuraMax 2** — value-premium alternative, quieter, multi-cat apartment fit.
3. **PetSafe ScoopFree Smart** — budget-proven, crystal-litter lane, roughly half the price of globes.
4. **Leo's Loo Too** — smart-home + UV-odor pick for Alexa/Google households.
5. **Neakasa M1 Open-Top** — the "my cat hates globes" pick; open-top at a mid-tier price.

Alternates held in reserve if narrowing changes in Phase 2: Litter-Robot EVO (budget entry into Whisker), homerunPET CS106 (Maine-Coon-sized households), Litter-Robot 5 Pro (absolute flagship).
