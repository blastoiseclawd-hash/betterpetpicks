## Final 5 — Locked picks for /low-tracking-cat-litter

Product pool diversity check: **clay × 2 + corn + wood + paper** (5 distinct material categories). Avoids overlap with LIVE `/best-cat-litter` top picks (BoxiePro Probiotic, Dr. Elsey's Ultra Unscented, Arm & Hammer Slide, Fresh Step Heavy Duty Odor Block). The LIVE hub's one World's Best pick is retained here because it's the only genuine low-tracking-first product that appeared in both editorial tier-1 sources; the page reframes the product around tracking-first decision logic vs the hub's secondary-role framing.

### Pick 1 — Best Mainstream Brand for Low Tracking
**Fresh Step Clean Paws Multi-Cat Scented Clumping Clay Cat Litter**
- Category: Clumping clay
- Why: Catster's 2026-03-08 Best Overall Non-Tracking pick. Ammonia-block technology (10-day odor claim per manufacturer). Particle shape + weight designed to stay in box. Mainstream availability (grocery + Chewy + Amazon).
- Fit: Clean-Space Manager who wants a name-brand unfussy answer. Budget tier ~$18-25 per 37 lb bag.
- Tradeoff: scented formula — some cats avoid strong fragrance; the unscented Clean Paws Febreze variant exists but was not the tier-1 editorial pick.
- Evidence: https://www.catster.com/lifestyle/best-non-tracking-cat-litter/

### Pick 2 — Best Natural Clumping (Corn-Based)
**World's Best Low Tracking & Dust Control Multiple Cat Unscented**
- Category: Corn granules (clumping)
- Why: Cats.com Best Clumping Natural 2026-01-01. Manufacturer-disclosed larger granule size = less paw-stickiness. Already established on LIVE `/best-cat-litter` as our Best Natural Alternative; low-tracking page reframes it as tracking-first pick rather than natural-clay-alternative pick.
- Fit: Clean-Space Manager who wants to step away from clay. Dust-sensitive cohort.
- Tradeoff: clumping is looser than heavy bentonite; slight odor retention tradeoff. Higher $/lb than mainstream clay.
- Evidence: https://cats.com/best-non-tracking-cat-litter + https://www.worldsbestcatlitter.com/blog/low-tracking-cat-litter-review/

### Pick 3 — Best Wood Pellet (With Clumping)
**Okocat Mini Pellets Unscented Clumping Wood Cat Litter**
- Category: Wood-fiber mini-pellets (clumping)
- Why: Cats.com Runner-up. Sustainably sourced wood fiber. Pellet shape minimizes paw-stickiness. Rare combination — most wood-pellet litters are non-clumping.
- Fit: Clean-Space Manager who wants renewable material + tracking reduction without giving up clumping.
- Tradeoff: pine-adjacent scent; some cats avoid wood-based textures on first exposure. Transition plan required.
- Evidence: https://cats.com/best-non-tracking-cat-litter

### Pick 4 — Best for Sensitive Cats / Extreme Low-Tracking
**Fresh News Unscented Non-Clumping Paper Cat Litter**
- Category: Recycled paper pellets (non-clumping)
- Why: Two-source editorial consensus — Catster's Best for Kittens + Cats.com's Best for Sensitive. Paper pellets are physically too heavy to stick to paws; "virtually no dust" claim. Best for post-surgical cats (no silica dust exposure), asthmatic cats, and households with respiratory-sensitive humans.
- Fit: Clean-Space Manager in a medically-sensitive household OR extreme-low-tracking-first use case.
- Tradeoff: non-clumping — scoop-and-dump pattern, higher change frequency (full change every 5-7 days). Lower odor control than clumping clay.
- Evidence: https://www.catster.com/lifestyle/best-non-tracking-cat-litter/ + https://cats.com/best-non-tracking-cat-litter

### Pick 5 — Best Budget Dust-Free Clay
**Cat's Pride Max Power Original Scent Multi-Cat Clumping Clay**
- Category: Clumping clay (heavy grain, low-dust)
- Why: Chewy editorial low-tracking category. 99% dust-free manufacturer claim. Lower $/lb than Fresh Step Clean Paws or Dr. Elsey's Premium — hits the budget-conscious single-cat or multi-cat home that wants clay clumping + real tracking reduction. Jug form factor easier to store.
- Fit: Clean-Space Manager on a budget; also secondary Household Operator fit (single-SKU multi-cat use).
- Tradeoff: scented (Original Scent); unscented variant exists but budget $/lb tier favors the scented SKU. Coarser granule vs Dr. Elsey's.
- Evidence: https://www.chewy.com/best/low-tracking-cat-litters_s2098582

## What we're NOT picking + why (cut-list)

- **Tuft & Paw Really Great Cat Litter** (Cats.com Best Overall) — subscription-only DTC channel, not mass retail, fails the BPP "reader can walk into a store / one-click Amazon" access test for broad Clean-Space Manager audience. Mention in body as premium alternative.
- **PetSafe ScoopFree Premium Crystal** (Catster + Cats.com) — locked into the ScoopFree proprietary system; not a drop-in litter swap. Silica-gel crystals also have cat-preference risk (some cats avoid crystal texture). FAQ mention only.
- **Boxiecat Probiotic Clumping Clay** (The Spruce Pets 2025 winner) — won on odor + clumping + tracking combined test, not tracking-first. Price tier higher than Fresh Step Clean Paws without a distinct tracking-first advantage. FAQ mention.
- **Dr. Elsey's Premium Clumping** (Chewy category pick) — the 40 lb bag is the closest variant; the Ultra Unscented variant is already the Best Value on LIVE `/best-cat-litter`. Including Dr. Elsey's Premium here risks reader confusion. Mention in body as the "if you want clay + dust-free absolute" fallback without making it a Final 5 pick.
- **Arm & Hammer Slide** (manufacturer-cited) — already LIVE `/best-cat-litter` Best for Multi-Cat pick. Avoid duplication.
- **Frisco Pine Pellet / Naturally Fresh Walnut / Feline Pine Original** — covered by Okocat Mini Pellets (more editorial-validated wood-pellet) + Fresh News (best paper/alternative fiber). Body mention as "if wood pellet is the material you want."
- **Tidy Cats 24/7 Performance / Tidy Cats Instant Action / Tidy Cats Breeze** — Tidy Cats covered by the category; Breeze is system-based and out of scope. Not included to keep the Final 5 narrative clean.
