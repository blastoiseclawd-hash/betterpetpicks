## Mat Types

1. **Two-layer honeycomb** — Top layer has holes, litter falls through to trapped bottom layer. Best trapping, hardest to vacuum.
2. **Silicone** — Flat with inner channels and raised edges. Easiest to clean, catches urine. Less trapping.
3. **Textured fabric** — Woven or debossed surface traps litter. Easiest to vacuum. Moderate trapping.
4. **Mesh/PVC coil** — Deep grooves capture litter. Durable. Hard to fully clean.
