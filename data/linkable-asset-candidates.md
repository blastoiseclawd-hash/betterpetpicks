# Linkable asset candidates — BetterPetPicks

*Discovery run: 2026-04-17. Site page count at run: 35. Active zone(s): 1 + 2 (patterns 1, 2, 6, 7 eligible; patterns 3 and 5 out-of-zone until page 60).*

## Method summary

- **Pass 1 (competitor backlink audit):** 3 competitors analyzed — thesprucepets.com, rover.com, petmd.com. Top 20 backlinked pages pulled per competitor (60 total high-RD pages reviewed; all with 193+ referring domains). Dominant pattern observed: pattern 4 (exhaustive reference, mostly breed profiles, 1000–2300 RD). Pattern 1 (lookup/reference) showed signal via Rover's hotel-pet-policy page (284 RD). Patterns 2, 3, 5 are visibly under-represented across authority pet sites — calculators and primary-research data studies simply do not dominate this niche's link equity. Ahrefs units consumed Pass 1: 1,080.
- **Pass 2 (pattern gap search):** WebSearch queries for cat-to-litter-box calculator, dog-bed sizing, coat-type / shedding diagram, litter-box placement checklist. Gaps identified: no neutral filterable cat-litter decision table; printable litter-placement checklists under-represented as citable assets; cross-brand neutral dog-bed sizing calculator missing (retailer tools only).
- **Pass 3 (scoring):** 11 candidates scored. 5 ≥ 7 (in-zone, ranked); 2 deferred (zone-3); 4 discarded (≤6 or fails no-fabrication / image-QC constraints). Top 5 injected into `guide-opportunities.md`.

## Pattern-level observations (for future runs)

- **Pattern 4 (exhaustive reference)** dominates high-RD counts for pet authority sites but almost always via breed / species profiles — not the niche BPP serves. BPP should not chase that pattern.
- **Pattern 1 (lookup)** is the niche's cleanest gap. Authority sites publish buying-guide articles, not filterable decision tables. Every top-5-ranked candidate above could be reframed as a lookup if gains were needed.
- **Pattern 2 (calculator)** is crowded in the cat-litter calculator sub-space but fragmented (7+ thin tools, no canonical). Combining multiple related calcs into one tool is defensible.
- **Pattern 3 (data study)** is universally under-produced in pet — high link reward if executed but execution cost is prohibitive without measurement infrastructure. Revisit after Phase 7 if lab/testing partner emerges.
- **Pattern 5 (annual state-of)** is genuinely empty in this niche. Worth revisiting once BPP has year-over-year traffic / product turnover data (realistically 18 months post-launch).
- **Pattern 6 (diagram)** is blocked by BPP's image-QC history. Do not build pattern-6 assets until either (a) human illustrator contract or (b) consistently passing AI image-QC gate.
- **Pattern 7 (downloadable)** is the cheapest pattern with proven-enough demand. Candidates 2 and 5 in the ranked list are both pattern 7; expect at least one to be built in the first 1-in-10 slot.
